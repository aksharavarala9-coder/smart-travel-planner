Important Travel Guidelines

🎒 Pack Smart

India's climate varies vastly. Always carry comfortable cottons for the plains, and heavy woolens if heading to the northern mountains.

🚰 Stay Hydrated

Always drink bottled or filtered water while traveling. Be mindful of street food hygiene to prevent stomach upsets.

🚆 Local Transit

Utilize metro systems, pre-paid taxis, or ride-hailing apps like Ola/Uber for efficient and secure intracity travel.
